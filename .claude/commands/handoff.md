   is nothing to commit, continue to step 2.

   structure, using today's date and the machine's hostname (run
   `hostname` to get it):

   Active branch:
   Roadmap position: <stage / task — "n/a" until ROADMAP has content>
   Done this session:
   In flight (half-finished):
   Decisions this session: <D-numbers + one-line gist, or "none">
   Blockers / open questions:
   Next steps (exact):
   Gotchas discovered:
3. Commit ("chore: handoff note") and push that too.
4. Confirm to me that everything is pushed and it is safe to walk away.
