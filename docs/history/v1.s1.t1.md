## Story

The data gate's cornerstone: FACTS.md enumerated the 47 world facts the
V1 engine must know across the five check families and the 26 optional
traveler parameters, each fact declaring its spine step, geographic
scope, freshness window, and a source slot for T2–T6 to fill. D-010
(global coverage via graded fallback ladders — no geography cut) and
D-011 (rich, nullable, tiered traveler vocabulary) rode in with it.
