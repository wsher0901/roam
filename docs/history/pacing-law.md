## What shipped

([D-009](../DECISIONS.md#d-009--2026-06--pacing-law-finish-first-flexible-cap))
· Recommendations needing the founder's attention are capped at

[PR #27](https://github.com/wsher0901/roam/pull/27)

## Story

D-009 replaced full menus with finish-first pacing: open lanes and
pending blocks outrank new tasks, at most "Now + one parallel slot"
asks for the founder's attention, "plate is full" is an honest answer,
and stopping is a valid next step — flexible in one direction only:
fully-specified no-decision work may still run as extra autonomous
lanes.
